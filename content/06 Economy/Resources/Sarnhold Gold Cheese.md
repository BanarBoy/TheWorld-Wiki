# Sarnhold Gold Cheese

**Resource Type:** Agricultural Product / Aged Cheese  
**Primary Region(s):** [[Gold Marches]]  
**Source Animal:** [[Goldhorn Cattle]]  
**Major Production Centers:** [[Sarnhold]] and surrounding ranchlands  
**Primary Uses:** Cuisine, trade export, noble banquets, merchant markets  
**Trade Value:** High / Durable Trade Good

---

# Overview

Sarnhold Gold is a renowned aged cheese produced in the ranching regions surrounding [[Sarnhold]] in the fertile plains of the [[Gold Marches]]. Made from the rich milk of [[Goldhorn Cattle]], the cheese is known for its firm texture, deep golden color, and complex savory flavor.

Because of its durability and long shelf life, Sarnhold Gold has become one of the most widely traded dairy products in Valecaryn. Large wheels of the cheese are transported along caravan routes and merchant shipping lanes, supplying cities, ports, and noble households across the continent.

Among chefs and merchants alike, Sarnhold Gold is valued not only as a staple food but also as a refined accompaniment to wine and other luxury goods. It is frequently served alongside prestigious wines from [[Merrowfen Vineyards]] or [[Ambervale Downs]].

---

# Geographic Origin

Sarnhold Gold originates from dairy farms and ranching estates across the [[Gold Marches]], where the wide grasslands provide ideal grazing conditions for [[Goldhorn Cattle]].

The region’s fertile pastures allow cattle to produce large quantities of rich, high-quality milk. Local dairy traditions have developed over generations, with ranchers and cheesemakers refining methods for aging and preserving the cheese.

The city of [[Sarnhold]] serves as the primary center of cheese production, aging, and distribution.

---

# Production

The cheese is produced using milk collected from Goldhorn cattle raised on the surrounding ranchlands.

Fresh milk is heated and curdled using traditional cheesemaking techniques. The curds are pressed into large wheels before being salted and stored in aging cellars.

Over time, the cheese develops its characteristic flavor and firm texture. Aging periods vary depending on the desired quality, with some wheels matured for many months before reaching market.

---

# Processing and Storage

After pressing, wheels of Sarnhold Gold are placed in stone aging cellars where temperature and humidity are carefully controlled.

These aging halls allow the cheese to mature slowly, developing its distinctive taste and deep golden color.

Because of its durability, Sarnhold Gold can be transported long distances without spoiling, making it ideal for trade caravans and merchant shipping.

---

# Economic Importance

Sarnhold Gold represents one of the most valuable dairy products produced in the [[Gold Marches]].

The cheese industry supports:

• dairy ranchers raising Goldhorn cattle  
• cheesemakers and cellar masters  
• livestock and dairy traders  
• merchant houses distributing food commodities

Large quantities of cheese move through trade hubs such as [[Sarnhold]] and [[Geldmere]] before being shipped across Valecaryn.

---

# Trade Networks

Sarnhold Gold travels widely through the continent’s trade networks.

Typical routes include:

Gold Marches → [[Sarnhold]] → [[Aureth Caldras]] → inland markets

Gold Marches → [[Geldmere]] → coastal shipping routes

The cheese is especially popular in cities with strong culinary traditions and in port cities that supply long-distance voyages.

---

# Cultural Significance

Sarnhold Gold is widely regarded as one of the finest cheeses produced in Valecaryn.

In noble households, the cheese is often served alongside wine as part of formal meals or celebratory feasts. Merchants and travelers commonly encounter it in taverns and markets across the continent.

For many residents of the Gold Marches, the cheese represents both regional pride and the agricultural prosperity of their homeland.

---

# Notable Locations

The primary center of cheese production and aging.

**[[Gold Marches]]**  
The ranchlands where the milk used for the cheese is produced.

**[[Geldmere]]**  
A major trade hub where large quantities of Sarnhold Gold pass through merchant markets.

---

# Rumors and Conflicts

Some merchants claim certain cheesemakers secretly age wheels of Sarnhold Gold far longer than usual, producing rare and extremely valuable vintages.

Others worry that disease among [[Goldhorn Cattle]] could threaten the dairy industry that sustains the region.

There are also occasional disputes between ranchers and cheesemakers over milk prices during poor grazing seasons.
