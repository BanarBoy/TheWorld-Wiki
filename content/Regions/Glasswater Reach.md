---

# Overview

The Glasswater Reach is the southwestern heart of Thalassar’s outward power—a region where river, sea, and industry converge to transform raw resources into global influence. Anchored by the great port of [[Seluqar]] and the river-gate city of [[Que’lehr]], the Reach is the final stage through which nearly all Thalassari wealth must pass before entering the wider world.

Defined by the lower basin of the [[Velashar River]] and its sprawling delta, the region is a complex network of waterways, docks, trade districts, and industrial zones. Inland goods arrive here in bulk—grain from the Zhar Basin, materials from the Khaldren Spine, spices and rare resources from the Masari Archipelago and Verdant Expanse—and are refined, sorted, and exported through Seluqar’s vast maritime infrastructure.

Unlike the rigid authority of Ashur-Taal or the decentralized chaos of the Masari islands, the Glasswater Reach is governed through layered systems of control. Royal authority, merchant syndicates, artisan guilds, and naval command all intersect here, creating a dynamic but tightly managed economic engine.

To outsiders, the Reach is dazzling—wealth, craftsmanship, and power on full display.  
To those who understand it, it is where decisions are made long before they are ever seen.

# Physical Geography

## Regional Position

The Glasswater Reach lies along the southwestern coast of Thalassar, centered on the mouth and lower basin of the [[Velashar River]].

It connects:

- inland Thalassari regions via river and caravan routes
- coastal maritime trade routes across the Gilded Expanse
- nearby island systems, including the [[Masari Archipelago]]

It serves as the natural endpoint of inland trade and the launching point for global commerce.

---

## Terrain

The region is characterized by:

- wide river deltas and branching waterways
- low coastal plains
- engineered harbors and dock systems
- artificial canals and trade channels

Natural terrain has been heavily modified to support:

- shipping infrastructure
- flood control
- urban expansion

---

## Hydrology

Water defines everything:

- the Velashar River feeds the entire region
- tidal influence shapes coastal movement
- canals and dredged channels maintain navigability

Waterways function as:

- transport routes
- economic arteries
- defensive features

---

## Climate

Spring  
Mild and humid; increased river flow.

Summer  
Warm, stable maritime conditions; peak trade season.

Autumn  
Busy export period as inland goods arrive.

Winter  
Moderate temperatures; reduced but steady trade activity.

Storms are occasional but rarely catastrophic due to natural harbor protections.
