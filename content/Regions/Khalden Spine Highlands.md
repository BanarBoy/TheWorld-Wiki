# **Khaldren Spine Highlands

**Continent:** [[Thalassar]]  
**Nation / Realm:** Highland Trade Confederacies  
**Region Type:** Mountains / Highlands  
**Climate Zone:** Alpine / Highland Temperate  
**Primary Terrain:** Rugged peaks, terraced slopes, forested ridgelines, deep valleys, river-cut corridors  
**Population:** Concentrated in major settlements and terraced zones; sparse in remote elevations  
**Major Cities:** [[Kelresh]], [[Dreshkul]], [[Houjoum]], [[Old Nerethis]], [[Kheled-Sar]]  
**Primary Economy:** Coffee cultivation (eastern highlands), mining and metallurgy, timber, river and caravan trade  
**Strategic Importance:** Controls eastern mountain passes, produces Deepsteel, and channels inland resources to coastal trade

---

# Overview

The Khaldren Spine Highlands form a vast and imposing mountain chain along eastern Thalassar, acting as both a natural barrier and a structured economic engine. Unlike the fertile Zhar Basin or the volatile Masari Archipelago, the Spine is defined by discipline, elevation, and the transformation of raw materials into refined, high-value goods.

The eastern highlands, centered around [[Kelresh]], are the most renowned region within the Spine. Here, terraced slopes and high-altitude conditions produce the world’s finest coffee, making the region indispensable to long-distance trade networks. Precision and consistency define life in these elevations.

Further south, the foothills give way to the industrial might of [[Dreshkul]], a dwarven-dominated mining stronghold. Here, the mountains are opened and reshaped through large-scale extraction, fueling the production of Khaldren Deepsteel and anchoring the Spine’s role as a metallurgical powerhouse.

At the point where the [[Kheled Flow]] meets the mountains, [[Kheled-Sar]] serves as a critical junction between water and stone. Acting as both a processing hub and logistical nexus, it channels raw materials from the mountains into river systems, linking highland industry to broader regional trade.

To the north, [[Old Nerethis]] sits near [[Lake Hoth]], one of the oldest settlements in the region, maintaining vital connections between highland routes and inland waterways. To the southeast, [[Houjoum]] anchors the Spine’s maritime access, exporting refined goods through the mouth of the [[Veldryn River]].

Together, these centers form a layered system of extraction, refinement, and distribution—where every part of the Spine contributes to a larger economic whole.

# Physical Geography

## Regional Position

The Khaldren Spine runs north to south along eastern Thalassar, forming a dominant geographic boundary.

It borders:

- the Zhar Basin to the west
- the Verdant Expanse to the south and southeast
- northern inland regions surrounding [[Lake Hoth]]
- coastal access routes via the [[Veldryn River]]

The Spine acts as both a defensive barrier and a controlled trade corridor, with movement funneled through passes and river systems such as the Kheled Flow.

---

## Terrain

The region is defined by dramatic verticality:

- steep mountain peaks and narrow ridgelines
- terraced eastern slopes shaped for agriculture
- forested highlands and coniferous zones
- deep valleys carved by rivers and geological activity

The southern foothills transition into more workable terrain, enabling large-scale mining around Dreshkul. The area around Kheled-Sar is defined by river-cut valleys, where water access allows for heavier settlement and industry.

---

## Hydrology

Water systems originate in the Spine and define its internal structure:

- the [[Kheled Flow]] descends from the mountains, creating a major industrial corridor at Kheled-Sar
- the [[Veldryn River]] provides southeastern drainage and access to the coast via Houjoum
- [[Lake Hoth]] anchors the northern region near Old Nerethis

These systems enable:

- transport of heavy materials
- settlement clustering
- industrial development in otherwise difficult terrain

Mild compared to lowlands; stable conditions for crop development.

Autumn  
Harvest season for coffee and highland crops.

- [[Kheled-Sar]] — river–mountain junction city where the [[Kheled Flow]] meets the Spine; industrial processing hub and logistical nexus

- [[Old Nerethis]] — northern settlement near [[Lake Hoth]]; historic trade connector linking highland routes to inland waterways

---

## Trade and Transportation

Trade in the Spine follows a structured flow:

- raw materials extracted in the southern and central mountains (Dreshkul)
- processed and redistributed through Kheled-Sar
- refined or cultivated goods produced in the east (Kelresh)
- exported via:
    - river routes (Veldryn → Houjoum)
    - caravan routes west toward Seluqar
    - northern inland routes via Old Nerethis

Movement is slow but controlled, increasing the value of all exported goods.
