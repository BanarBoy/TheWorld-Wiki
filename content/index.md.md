# Valecaryn Index

---

# Overview

Valecaryn is a unified maritime kingdom defined by the balance between **crown authority, noble power, and interconnected trade systems**. Its strength lies not in any single institution, but in the coordination of houses, routes, and resources that together sustain its dominance.
