# Overview

Lungmei Sap is a refined narcotic extract derived from the milky fluid of a cultivated flowering plant grown in the irrigated lowlands of Kiyora. Unlike crude substances, it is processed into a smooth, stable form through controlled drying and refinement, producing a substance known for its heavy, enveloping sedative effects.

When used in measured quantities, Lungmei Sap induces deep physical relaxation, suppresses pain, and creates a slow, dreamlike mental state. Its effects are gradual and sustained, making it particularly valuable in medical contexts where prolonged sedation or relief is required.

However, its potency comes with risk. Improper use can lead to dependency, and overconsumption can dull both body and mind over time. Because of this, Lungmei is tightly regulated, treated not as a common indulgence but as a **controlled substance of both necessity and danger**.
