# Overview

Icefang Pike is a large, cold-water predator fish found in the rivers, lakes, and coastal inlets of northern Eryndor. Known for its firm, dense flesh and high fat content, it is one of the most important protein sources in the region and a cornerstone of both fresh and preserved food systems.

The fish takes its name from its sharp, protruding teeth and aggressive feeding behavior, as well as the icy environments it inhabits. Its meat is clean, slightly rich, and highly suited to preservation, making it especially valuable in Frostcure processes.

Across Eryndor, Icefang Pike serves a dual role:

• a staple fresh catch in fishing communities  
• a primary source material for long-term preserved foods

> Where the water runs cold,  
> Icefang follows.

# Geographic Distribution

Icefang Pike thrives in:

• the interconnected waterways of the [[Threewaters]] basin  
• cold coastal zones around [[Stormhaven]] and [[Frostgate]]  
• inland rivers and lakes throughout [[Ulthridge]] and [[Severin]]

These regions provide:

• cold, oxygen-rich waters  
• strong current systems  
• abundant smaller fish populations

The species prefers:

• deep channels  
• shaded river systems  
• glacially fed lakes and estuaries

Because these conditions are widespread in northern Eryndor, Icefang Pike is:

• regionally abundant  
• ecologically dominant in some waterways

# Production

Icefang Pike is harvested through both river and coastal fishing practices.

---

### Fishing Methods

• net fishing in river channels  
• line fishing in deeper waters  
• trap systems in narrow waterways

Coastal regions (Stormhaven / Frostgate):

• small fleet operations  
• nearshore netting

Inland regions (Ulthridge / Severin):

• river-based fishing  
• seasonal lake harvesting

---

### Yield Characteristics

• medium to large size  
• high meat-to-bone ratio  
• consistent availability in colder seasons

Fishing is:

• steady but environmentally dependent  
• more reliable in colder months  
• tied to water conditions and migration patterns

# Processing and Transport

---

### Fresh Use

Fresh Icefang Pike is:

• cleaned and prepared quickly  
• consumed locally in fishing communities  
• used in stews, grilling, and roasting

---

### Preservation (Critical Role)

A large portion of the catch is processed into:

• **Icefang Pike Cure** (Frostcure product)  
• salted and air-dried fillets  
• lightly smoked preservation cuts

This makes it:

• transportable  
• long-lasting  
• suitable for trade and provisioning

---

### Transport

Icefang Pike products move through:

• river trade networks in [[Threewaters]]  
• coastal shipping routes from [[Stormhaven]] and [[Frostgate]]  
• inland caravans from [[Ulthridge]] and [[Severin]]
