**Resource Type:** Medicinal / Alchemical / Trade Good  
**Primary Region(s):** [[Kiyora]] — produced in apothecary centers within the **Minghe**, **Qinghe**, and **Fenghe** river basins near [[Minghu]], [[Qinghe]], and [[Fenghai]]  
**Major Production Centers:** Shenhua-affiliated workshops in [[Minghu]] (Minghe basin), agricultural processing near [[Qinghe]] (Qinghe basin), refinement houses in [[Fenghai]] (Fenghe basin)  
**Controlling Groups:** Shenhua Circles, licensed apothecaries, herbalist guilds, state-regulated medicinal distributors  
**Primary Uses:** Wound treatment, burn relief, inflammation reduction, skin restoration  
**Trade Value:** Common → Valuable (refined forms)

---

# Overview

Yuhua Balm, often referred to as the “Jade Bloom Salve,” is one of the most widely used medicinal preparations in Kiyora. Recognizable by its pale green color and smooth, cooling texture, it is applied directly to the skin to treat minor wounds, burns, and irritation.

Unlike aggressive or rapid-acting treatments, Yuhua Balm works through stabilization and gradual restoration. It reduces heat and inflammation while encouraging the body’s natural healing processes to proceed without disruption. This aligns closely with the broader Shenhua philosophy: not to force recovery, but to guide it toward balance.

Its formulation is based on a refined combination of lotus-derived compounds, botanical oils, and carefully processed mineral elements. The result is a salve that is both gentle and reliable, suitable for daily use across all levels of society—from laborers and soldiers to scholars and nobility.

Though common in its basic form, higher-quality Yuhua Balm—clearer in color, smoother in consistency, and more precisely balanced—is considered a mark of skilled preparation and commands greater value in urban markets.

# Production

Yuhua Balm is produced through a layered refinement process.

---

### Base Extraction

• Lotus Root and related plants are processed into cooling extracts  
• oils are infused with botanical compounds  
• raw materials are filtered to remove impurities

---

### Integration

• plant extracts and oils are blended in precise ratios  
• mineral components are added in controlled quantities  
• mixtures are heated and cooled to achieve stability

---

### Refinement

• balm is filtered for smoothness  
• texture and consistency are standardized  
• batches are tested for effectiveness and purity

Production is:

• moderately scalable  
• highly dependent on ingredient quality  
• refined through practiced technique
