# Overview

Reedgold is a tall, fibrous river reed cultivated across the floodplains and wetlands of Thalassar, combining the structure of papyrus with the sugar-rich core of cane. It grows in dense, shimmering stands along riverbanks and irrigation terraces, its golden stalks bending in thick clusters that signal both abundance and control.

Once processed, Reedgold yields two primary forms: a **golden crystallized sugar** with a warm, caramel note, and a **thick, dark syrup** prized for its depth and intensity. These forms define much of Thalassari cuisine, from common street food to elite culinary craft, making Reedgold one of the most culturally and economically influential resources on the continent.

Unlike many crops, Reedgold is not confined to a single region or city. It is a **network crop**, grown across the entire Zhar river system and extending into the Verdant Expanse. Its production binds together multiple settlements, each contributing differently to cultivation, processing, or distribution.

---

# Geographic Distribution

Reedgold grows throughout:

- the floodplains of the [[Zhar Basin]] along the **Zhar River** and its tributaries
- the wetland zones of [[Sarith Fen]]
- irrigated agricultural belts around [[Tal'mir Fields]]
- river-connected settlements such as [[Tarzhir]] and [[Zharakel]]
- jungle-edge waterways in the [[Verdant Expanse]] near [[Irathel]]

It thrives in:

- nutrient-rich silt deposited by seasonal flooding
- humid, water-saturated soils
- low-elevation river corridors

The Zhar Basin produces the most consistent and controlled yields, while Verdant Expanse Reedgold is more variable—often darker, richer, and less uniform.

---

# Production

Reedgold cultivation is distributed across a **tiered agricultural system**:

- **Tal'mir Fields** → large-scale, structured cultivation
- **Sarith Fen** → wetland-grown Reedgold with deeper flavor
- **Tarzhir** → coordination hub for harvesting and redistribution
- **Zharakel** → administrative oversight and processing control
- **Irathel** → frontier harvesting and expansion into jungle zones

Production in the Zhar Basin is tightly controlled:

- irrigation timing is dictated by Azhariel authorities
- harvest cycles align with flood patterns
- labor is organized at scale

In contrast, Verdant Expanse production is:

- decentralized
- less predictable
- often controlled by local groups

---

# Processing and Transport

Reedgold is highly perishable once harvested and must be processed quickly.

Primary processing occurs in:

- [[Zharakel]] — large-scale refinement and crystallization
- [[Tarzhir]] — redistribution and mid-tier processing
- [[Irathel]] — frontier processing for jungle-grown Reedgold

Processing stages:

1. crushing stalks to extract juice
2. boiling and reduction
3. separation into:
    - **Golden Reed Sugar** (refined, export-grade)
    - **Reedgold Syrup** (dense, dark, regionally favored)

Transport follows the river:

- barges move goods down the Zhar system toward [[Port Iskene]] and the coast
- caravans carry refined goods toward the [[Glasswater Reach]]
- exports move through [[Seluqar]] into global markets

---

# Economic Importance

Reedgold is a **keystone resource** in Thalassar.

It:

- supports massive agricultural labor systems
- enhances the value of Banar, coffee, and spice-based cuisine
- fuels both everyday consumption and elite markets

Cities dependent on Reedgold include:

- [[Seluqar]] (refinement and export)
- [[Kelresh]] (coffee pairing and elite consumption)
- [[Zharakel]] and [[Tarzhir]] (production and coordination)

It is both a staple and a multiplier—its presence increases the value of nearly every other food resource.

---

# Controlling Powers

Reedgold is controlled through **water and processing**, not just land.

- the **Azhariel Clan** controls irrigation and large-scale production in the Zhar Basin
- river temple authorities regulate planting and harvest cycles
- local groups operate in the Verdant Expanse with less oversight
- Seluqar merchants control high-end refinement and export pricing

Control of refinement—especially crystal sugar—is a major source of economic power.

---

# Trade Networks

Reedgold moves through interconnected systems:

- river transport from Zhar Basin to [[Port Iskene]]
- redistribution through [[Que’lehr]] into Seluqar systems
- maritime export via [[Seluqar]]

It also moves:

- inland through caravan routes
- north toward [[Old Nerethis]] and lake networks
- southeast via the [[Veldryn River]] through [[Houjoum]]

Globally, Reedgold is:

- a luxury sweetener
- a culinary signature of Thalassar
- a high-value trade good

---

# Cultural Significance

Reedgold defines sweetness in Thalassar.

It is used in:

- everyday cooking
- street foods
- ceremonial dishes
- elite cuisine

It is inseparable from:

- Banar dishes
- spiced preparations
- Kelresh coffee culture

Sweetness is not incidental—it is **constructed and layered**.

# Dangers and Limitations

- dependent on stable flood cycles
- highly perishable pre-processing
- vulnerable to irrigation disruption
- labor-intensive harvesting

Disruption of the Zhar system can cause widespread economic instability.
