**Resource Type:** Livestock  
**Primary Region(s):** Southern Eryndor — the fertile lowlands and river-fed plains of the **Almaraine Basin**, south of [[Fort Londor]], including [[Brannor]], [[Gildhall]], [[Belthorn]], [[Alcienne]], [[Ulthridge]], and extending to [[Corvaire]]  
**Major Production Centers:** Ranching belts along the **Almaraine River**, grazing lands near [[Brannor]] and [[Gildhall]], and southern pastures surrounding [[Belthorn]] and [[Alcienne]]  
**Controlling Groups:** Ranching families, pastoral estates, livestock guilds, regional agricultural authorities  
**Primary Uses:** Meat (Fieldcut, Rimecut Strips, Stonehams), milk (cheese production), hides (Fellhide)  
**Trade Value:** Common → Strategic (derivative goods)

---

# Overview

Broadhorn Cattle are the dominant large livestock of southern Eryndor, forming the foundation of the region’s agricultural economy. Raised across the fertile lowlands of the Almaraine Basin, they thrive in conditions that allow for sustained grazing, larger herds, and consistent production.

Broad-bodied and slow-growing, Broadhorns are valued for their high yield and versatility. They produce abundant meat, rich milk, and thick, durable hides, supporting industries ranging from food production to leatherworking. Unlike the hardier but lower-yield Ridgeback Sheep of the north, Broadhorns depend on stable land and favorable conditions—but reward that stability with scale.

The Almaraine River ties this entire system together. From the inland pastures of [[Ulthridge]] through [[Brannor]], [[Gildhall]], and [[Belthorn]], cattle production, dairy processing, and trade move steadily downstream toward [[Corvaire]], where goods are distributed outward.

> Where the north prepares for scarcity,  
> the south produces enough to sustain the rest.

# Geographic Distribution

Broadhorn Cattle are concentrated in:

• the river-fed plains of the **Almaraine Basin**  
• grazing lands surrounding [[Brannor]] and [[Gildhall]]  
• fertile lowlands of [[Belthorn]] and [[Alcienne]]  
• the wider [[Ulthridge]] region extending toward [[Corvaire]]

These regions provide:

• longer growing seasons  
• consistent water access via the Almaraine  
• nutrient-rich grasses and pastureland

As a result:

• herd sizes are largest in the south  
• production is most stable along the river corridor

North of [[Fort Londor]]:

• grazing becomes less reliable  
• herd sizes diminish  
• Ridgeback Sheep replace cattle as the dominant livestock

# Production

Broadhorn cattle ranching is structured, land-dependent, and production-focused.

---

### Grazing Practices

• managed pasture systems  
• seasonal rotation to preserve land  
• reliance on open plains and river-fed grasslands

Ranchers prioritize:

• herd growth and long-term stability  
• meat and milk yield  
• sustainable use of grazing land

---

### Outputs

Broadhorn Cattle are one of the most productive livestock sources in Eryndor.

They provide:

• **Meat** — fresh (Fieldcut) in the south, preserved (Rimecut, Stonehams) for transport  
• **Milk** — the foundation of southern cheesemaking traditions  
• **Hides** — primary input for Fellhide leather

Compared to Ridgeback Sheep:

• higher output  
• greater economic impact  
• lower environmental resilience

---

# Processing and Transport

---

### Meat

• consumed fresh in southern regions  
• preserved via Frostcure for export  
• processed in settlements along the Almaraine corridor

---

### Dairy

• processed locally into cheese  
• surplus converted into more stable products for transport

---

### Hides

• sent to tanning centers for Fellhide production  
• often processed near ranching regions or trade hubs

---

### Transport

Broadhorn products move along the **Almaraine River corridor**:

• inland grazing zones → processing settlements  
• mid-river regions → distribution hubs  
• downstream → [[Corvaire]] for export

This creates a unified system where:

> production, processing, and trade all follow the same path

---

# Economic Importance

Broadhorn Cattle are central to Eryndor’s economy.

They:

• supply the majority of meat and dairy  
• sustain southern agricultural wealth  
• support preservation and trade industries  
• feed both rural and urban populations

The Almaraine Basin acts as:

> the primary production engine of Eryndor

---

# Controlling Powers

Control is more structured than in highland systems.

Primary actors include:

• ranching families and large pastoral estates  
• livestock guilds  
• regional agricultural authorities

Power is determined by:

• herd size  
• land ownership  
• access to water and grazing routes

---

# Trade Networks

Broadhorn-derived goods are distributed across:

• northern and central Eryndor  
• Valecaryn trade routes  
• Seluqar export markets via [[Corvaire]]

They are traded as:

• fresh meat (regional)  
• preserved meats (long-distance)  
• cheese products  
• hides and leather goods

---

# Cultural Significance

Broadhorn Cattle represent:

• productivity  
• stability  
• southern prosperity

In contrast to the harsher north, they are associated with:

• fuller meals  
• greater dietary variety  
• agricultural success

In many southern communities, herd size reflects:

> capacity and influence, not survival

---

# Dangers and Limitations

• dependence on stable grazing land  
• vulnerability to drought or flooding  
• disease spread in large herds  
• overgrazing risks

Additionally:

• disruption of the Almaraine River system would impact the entire network  
• loss of pastureland can collapse regional production
