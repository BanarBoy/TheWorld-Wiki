# Overview

Khaldren Deepsteel is the finest structural metal produced in Thalassar, forged exclusively in the industrial forges of [[Dreshkul]] at the base of the [[Khaldren Spine]]. It is not defined by rare ore alone, but by a **system-dependent process** that combines specialized materials, controlled environments, and tightly held forging knowledge.

At its core, Deepsteel begins with high-quality iron ore containing trace elements that respond exceptionally well to carbon hardening. What elevates it beyond conventional steel is the use of **Spineflint** as a flux during smelting, allowing for near-total impurity removal and the creation of an exceptionally pure metal base.

The result is a steel that:

• holds a superior edge  
• resists fatigue and stress fractures  
• maintains structural integrity under extreme conditions

Deepsteel forms the backbone of Thalassar’s weapons, tools, and naval fittings, with the highest grades reserved for Seluqar fleets and elite forces.

> Ore gives strength.  
> Spineflint gives purity.  
> Dreshkul gives form.

# Production

Deepsteel is produced through a controlled, multi-stage process unique to Dreshkul.

---

## 1. Ore Selection

• only high-grade ore with correct trace composition is accepted  
• inconsistent ore is rejected immediately

---

## 2. Spineflint Smelting (Critical Stage)

• Spineflint is introduced as a flux  
• aggressively binds impurities into slag  
• produces an exceptionally pure metal bloom

👉 This is the defining step

---

## 3. Carbon Hardening

• controlled carbon infusion  
• precise temperature cycling  
• balances strength and flexibility

---

## 4. Deepforge Working

• repeated folding and hammering  
• structural alignment within the metal  
• stress distribution refinement

---

## 5. Final Tempering

• controlled cooling cycles  
• locks in durability and resilience

---

Production is:

• slow  
• highly specialized  
• tightly limited
