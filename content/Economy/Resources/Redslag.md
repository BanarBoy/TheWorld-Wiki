# Redslag

**Resource Type:** Industrial / Byproduct Compound  
**Primary Region(s):** [[Blackspine Mountain Foothills]]  
**Major Production Centers:** [[Khordun-Zhal]]  
**Controlling Groups:** Zharg forge syndicates, Brathnul salvage crews, Council of Foundries  
**Primary Uses:** Alloy strengthening, industrial accelerant, demolition charges, experimental metallurgy  
**Trade Value:** Valuable / Strategic

---

# Overview

Redslag is a volatile, high-density metallic byproduct produced during the deep-forge processes of [[Khordun-Zhal]]. Formed when impurities and excess material are burned out under extreme Zharg heat conditions, it was once considered waste—until its properties were better understood.

Unlike ordinary slag, Redslag retains a form of **residual reactivity**, the result of incomplete stabilization during the forging process. When properly handled and refined, it can be reintroduced into metalworking systems to enhance structural strength or accelerate bonding between materials. In less controlled applications, it can produce violent and unpredictable reactions.

Over time, Redslag has become a valuable secondary export. While it lacks the prestige of Blackspine Steel, its versatility and potency make it highly sought after by engineers, military forces, and experimental craftsmen across [[Valecaryn]].

Its existence reflects a core truth of Khordun-Zhal:

> nothing produced in the mountain is truly waste

---

# Geographic Distribution

Redslag does not occur naturally and cannot be mined.

It is generated exclusively within:

• high-temperature forge systems  
• slag runoff channels  
• deep smelting operations in [[Khordun-Zhal]]

Its formation depends on:

• extreme heat thresholds unique to Zharg forging  
• specific mineral compositions found in Blackspine ore  
• controlled but incomplete separation of impurities

Attempts to replicate Redslag outside Khordun-Zhal have failed due to insufficient heat conditions and lack of proper material composition.

---

# Production

Redslag is produced as a **secondary output** of large-scale forging.

During smelting:

• raw ore is subjected to extreme heat  
• impurities are forced out as molten waste  
• portions of this material stabilize into Redslag instead of inert slag

Collection is hazardous and typically handled by:

• Zharg forge crews (initial separation)  
• Brathnul salvage teams (cooling, extraction, and containment)

Production scale is:

• continuous but inconsistent  
• dependent on forge output and material composition

Not all slag becomes Redslag—only a fraction develops the necessary properties.

---

# Processing and Transport

Raw Redslag is unstable and must be processed before transport.

Processing includes:

• controlled cooling cycles to prevent spontaneous reaction  
• grinding into usable powder or compacted forms  
• containment in reinforced, heat-resistant vessels

Transport is tightly regulated:

• sealed containers carried via secured caravan routes  
• limited shipment sizes due to volatility  
• specialized handling protocols enforced by contract

Improperly handled Redslag can:

• ignite  
• fracture containers  
• react violently under pressure

As a result, it is rarely traded openly and never handled casually.

# Economic Importance

Redslag occupies a unique position in the economy of [[Valecaryn]].

It is not essential—but it provides a **distinct advantage**.

Key uses include:

• strengthening alloys during reforging  
• accelerating industrial forging processes  
• controlled demolition in mining and warfare  
• experimental applications in advanced metallurgy

Industries that rely on it:

• military engineering  
• large-scale construction  
• specialized weapons manufacturing

It is particularly valued by those seeking:

> an edge that standard materials cannot provide

---

# Controlling Powers

Redslag is controlled entirely within [[Khordun-Zhal]].

Key groups include:

• **Zharg Forge Syndicates** — oversee initial production and separation  
• **Brathnul Salvage Crews** — manage recovery, refinement, and containment  
• **Council of Foundries** — regulates export volume and use

Because it originates as a byproduct, control is less centralized than Blackspine Steel—but still tightly managed.

Unauthorized removal is considered a serious offense.

---

# Trade Networks

Redslag is exported in limited quantities to:

• industrial centers within [[Valecaryn]]  
• military contractors and engineering groups  
• select foreign buyers through controlled agreements

Trade characteristics:

• contract-based distribution  
• restricted access  
• high demand relative to supply

Roskul traders handle logistics but operate under strict oversight.

---

# Cultural Significance

Within Khordun-Zhal, Redslag represents:

> risk, waste, and opportunity combined

Among Zharg and Brathnul workers, it carries a more practical meaning:

• proof of a successful forge run  
• a reminder of the dangers of the work  
• a resource earned through hazard

It is not celebrated—but it is respected.

---

# Dangers and Limitations

Redslag is inherently unstable.

Primary risks include:

• explosive reactions under improper handling  
• ignition when exposed to heat or pressure fluctuations  
• structural damage if used incorrectly in construction

Limitations:

• cannot replace primary metals like Blackspine Steel  
• requires skilled handling to be useful  
• dangerous in unregulated environments

Its value is directly tied to:

> the skill of the person using it
