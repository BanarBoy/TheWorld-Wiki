# Ridgefall Roast

**Resource Type:** Agricultural Product / Luxury Coffee  
**Primary Region(s):** Kelresh Highlands, Thalassar  
**Major Production Centers:** [[Ridgefall Estate]]  
**Controlling Groups:** [[House Allacroix]]  
**Primary Uses:** Coffee houses, noble households, merchant exchanges, scholarly circles  
**Trade Value:** Luxury Import

---

# Overview

Ridgefall Roast is a renowned coffee imported to Valecaryn from the overseas estates of [[House Allacroix]] in the highlands of Kelresh. Cultivated on the terraced slopes surrounding [[Ridgefall Estate]], the beans are known for their rich aroma, dark body, and distinctive bitterness balanced by subtle sweetness.

Because coffee cannot be grown in the temperate climate of Valecaryn, Ridgefall Roast arrives entirely through maritime trade. The beans are shipped across the seas by merchant fleets aligned with House Allacroix and distributed through major trade hubs such as [[Merrowfen]], [[Geldmere]], and [[Lethryn Prime]].

Over the past generation, Ridgefall Roast has become increasingly fashionable among merchants, scholars, and political elites. Coffee houses serving the drink have appeared in many major cities, where merchants negotiate contracts, scholars debate philosophy, and political agents quietly exchange information.

Though still considered a luxury beverage, the growing popularity of Ridgefall Roast has begun to reshape social life in Valecaryn’s urban centers.

---

# Geographic Origin

Ridgefall Roast originates in the mountainous highlands of Kelresh in the southwestern reaches of Thalassar.

The region’s environment is well suited for coffee cultivation due to several natural advantages:

• warm tropical climate with steady rainfall  
• high elevations that slow bean maturation and deepen flavor  
• volcanic soils rich in minerals  
• steep terraced hillsides ideal for coffee groves

The plantations surrounding [[Ridgefall Estate]] produce beans widely regarded as among the finest exported from the region.

---

# Production

Coffee plants are cultivated across terraced plantations on the slopes surrounding Ridgefall Estate.

During harvest seasons, workers gather ripe coffee cherries by hand. The fruit is then processed to extract the beans, which are washed, dried, and sorted before roasting.

The distinctive **Ridgefall Roast** style is produced through a carefully controlled roasting process that darkens the beans while preserving their aromatic oils. This method creates the strong flavor profile that has become associated with Allacroix coffee.

Once roasted, the beans are sealed in trade chests to preserve freshness during transport.

---

# Processing and Storage

After roasting, Ridgefall beans are packed into sealed wooden chests lined with protective materials to prevent moisture damage during the long sea voyage.

The coffee is transported by merchant fleets from Kelresh to Valecaryn, where it is stored in warehouses in major port cities before being distributed to inland markets.

Because freshness affects flavor, merchants often move shipments quickly through trade networks.

---

# Economic Importance

Although produced overseas, Ridgefall Roast has become an important luxury import within Valecaryn’s economy.

The trade supports:

• plantation workers in Kelresh  
• merchant fleets transporting the coffee  
• warehouse and trade houses in port cities  
• coffee houses and specialty merchants in urban centers

For [[House Allacroix]], the coffee trade provides a valuable international revenue stream alongside their famous wine estates.

---

# Trade Networks

Ridgefall Roast travels along major maritime trade routes between Thalassar and Valecaryn.

Typical trade flow:

Kelresh → merchant fleets → [[Merrowfen]] → inland trade networks

Major markets include:

• [[Merrowfen]]  
• [[Geldmere]]  
• [[Aureth Caldras]]  
• [[Lethryn Prime]]

Coffee houses and merchant guild halls are the primary consumers of the product.

---

# Cultural Significance

Coffee drinking has grown steadily in popularity among merchants, scholars, and administrators.

Unlike wine, which is traditionally associated with feasts and noble gatherings, coffee is often consumed in settings focused on discussion, negotiation, and study.

Coffee houses serving Ridgefall Roast have become important social hubs where merchants negotiate trade agreements, scholars debate ideas, and travelers exchange news from distant lands.

---

# Notable Locations

**[[Ridgefall Estate]]**  
The overseas plantation estate in Kelresh where Ridgefall Roast coffee is cultivated and processed.

**[[Merrowfen]]**  
The primary entry port and trade center through which Ridgefall coffee enters Valecaryn.

**[[Geldmere]]**  
A major financial city where coffee houses have become popular meeting places for merchants and financiers.

---

# Rumors and Conflicts

Some merchants claim rival houses are attempting to establish competing coffee plantations elsewhere in Thalassar.

Others suspect that smugglers occasionally divert shipments of Ridgefall Roast to avoid Merrowfen trade tariffs.

There are also rumors that experimental plantations near Ridgefall Estate may soon produce even stronger or rarer varieties of coffee.
