# Overview

Emberscale is a rare, scale-like mineral formation found within the active volcanic regions surrounding the ruins of [[Old Sylrae]]. Appearing as layered, iridescent fragments embedded within cooled lava and ash deposits, it bears a striking resemblance to overlapping scales—hence its name.

Unlike standard volcanic materials such as [[Shenjin Glass]], Emberscale exhibits unusual internal structure and behavior. It retains heat longer than expected, reacts unpredictably when refined, and appears to enhance or stabilize certain alchemical processes when used in controlled quantities.

Its origin is uncertain. While officially classified as a geological anomaly, many believe Emberscale is connected to the ancient Sylrae civilization—or to something that existed alongside it. Fragments recovered closer to the ruins often display more consistent and potent properties, reinforcing the belief that its formation is not purely natural.

Within Kiyora, Emberscale is treated with caution. It is not widely used, and its handling is restricted to highly trained practitioners. Where Shenjin Glass represents precision, Emberscale represents potential—power that has not yet been fully understood.

# Production

Emberscale is extracted under extreme conditions.

---

### Extraction

• located within unstable volcanic terrain  
• removed from rock using careful fracturing techniques  
• requires precise timing between eruptions

---

### Selection

• fragments are sorted by structure and coloration  
• stable pieces are separated from volatile ones  
• high-quality Emberscale is rare and carefully cataloged

---

### Risk Factors

• extreme heat and toxic gases

Production is:

• highly dangerous
