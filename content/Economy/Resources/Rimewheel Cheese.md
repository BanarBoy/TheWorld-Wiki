# Overview

Rimewheel is a hard, aged cheese produced from the milk of highland sheep and goats across Eryndor’s colder regions. Known for its dense texture, sharp flavor, and long shelf life, it is one of the most widely consumed and exported food products in the north.

Formed into large, durable wheels and aged in cold stone environments, Rimewheel develops a firm body with subtle crystalline structures that give it a distinctive bite. Its flavor is rich and nutty with a clean, slightly sharp finish, shaped in part by the mineral-rich grazing lands surrounding [[Lake Morra]].

Unlike softer cheeses of warmer regions, Rimewheel is built for endurance. It travels well, stores for long periods, and remains reliable even under harsh conditions, making it a staple for soldiers, sailors, and travelers alike.

> It does not spoil easily.  
> It does not soften quickly.  
> It endures.

---

# Geographic Distribution

Rimewheel production is concentrated in:

• the grazing lands surrounding [[Lake Morra]]  
• upland pastures near [[Ravenfjord]]  
• foothills and valleys beneath the [[Crown of Winter]]

These regions provide:

• cold, stable climates ideal for aging  
• mineral-rich grasses influenced by glacial runoff into [[Lake Morra]]  
• natural stone environments suitable for storage

The consistent low temperatures slow the aging process, allowing:

• deeper flavor development  
• tighter texture formation  
• longer preservation

# Production

Rimewheel begins with carefully managed dairy herds.

---

### Milk Sources

• primarily sheep and goats adapted to highland conditions  
• rich, high-fat milk influenced by cold grazing environments  
• seasonal variation tied to pasture quality around [[Lake Morra]]

---

### Cheesemaking Process

• milk is heated and cultured  
• curds are cut, pressed, and shaped into large wheels  
• wheels are salted—often using **Glacier Salt**  
• initial drying occurs before aging

Production is:

• steady but not industrialized  
• widely distributed across highland communities  
• rooted in generational knowledge

# Processing and Transport

---

### Aging (Critical Stage)

Rimewheel is aged in:

• cold stone vaults near [[Ravenfjord]]  
• mountain cellars overlooking [[Lake Morra]]  
• subterranean storage chambers in highland settlements

Aging conditions:

• low temperature  
• controlled airflow  
• minimal moisture fluctuation

This produces:

• dense structure  
• crystalline texture  
• long shelf stability

---

### Transport

Rimewheel is transported as:

• full wheels for long-distance trade  
• cut wedges for local markets

It moves through:

• caravan routes from [[Lake Morra]] and [[Ravenfjord]]  
• maritime trade via northern ports  
• inland distribution across Eryndor and beyond

Its durability makes it ideal for:

• long journeys  
• military supply chains  
• naval provisioning

# Economic Importance

Rimewheel is a **core food resource** in Eryndor.

It:

• sustains rural dairy economies around [[Lake Morra]]

[[Ravenfjord]] acts as a major export hub, while inland regions depend on it as a daily staple.

It occupies a unique position:

> both survival food and trade good
