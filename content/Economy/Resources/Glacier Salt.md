# Overview

Glacier Salt is a mineral-rich salt formed from the slow accumulation and evaporation of glacial runoff across Eryndor’s cold highland basins. Unlike common sea salt, it carries trace minerals from ancient ice and mountain stone, giving it a sharper, cleaner taste and exceptional preservative properties.

Harvested from exposed salt beds and evaporation flats left behind by retreating glacial waters, Glacier Salt is both abundant in certain regions and difficult to extract consistently. Its quality depends heavily on location, mineral composition, and harvesting conditions.

In Eryndor, Glacier Salt is not considered a luxury—it is a **necessity**. It underpins food preservation systems, supports long-distance travel, and enables survival through harsh winters where fresh resources are limited.

> Grain feeds you today.  
> Salt keeps you alive tomorrow.

# Geographic Distribution

Glacier Salt forms in:

• runoff basins beneath the [[Crown of Winter]]  
• mineral-rich valleys feeding into [[Lake Morra]]  
• seasonal salt flats exposed during warmer months

These areas are shaped by:

• glacial meltwater carrying dissolved minerals  
• slow evaporation in cold, dry air  
• repeated seasonal accumulation

The best deposits are found where:

• water flow is steady but not turbulent  
• mineral concentration is high  
• evaporation cycles are consistent

Different regions produce slightly different salt profiles, ranging from:

• sharp and crystalline  
• to denser, mineral-heavy forms

# Production

Glacier Salt extraction is labor-intensive and highly dependent on environmental conditions.

---

### Extraction Methods

• harvesting salt crusts from exposed flats  
• channeling runoff into shallow evaporation basins  
• cutting and collecting mineral-rich deposits

Work is often:

• seasonal  
• physically demanding  
• conducted in harsh, exposed environments

---

### Refinement

Minimal refinement is preferred.

Steps include:

• breaking and sorting salt crystals  
• removing impurities  
• drying and stabilizing for storage

Higher-grade Glacier Salt is:

• more uniform  
• cleaner in taste  
• more effective for preservation

# Processing and Transport

---

### Processing

Glacier Salt is typically:

• left coarse for preservation use  
• ground or refined for culinary applications  
• sometimes compressed into transport blocks

---

### Transport

Glacier Salt is transported via:

• caravan routes from highland basins  
• distribution networks centered around [[Lake Morra]]  
• trade routes connecting to cities such as [[Ravenfjord]]

Because of its importance, it is:

• moved in bulk  
• carefully protected from moisture  
• stored in sealed containers or lined crates

---

# Economic Importance

Glacier Salt is one of the most strategically important resources in Eryndor.

It:

• enables long-term food storage  
• supports military provisioning  
• sustains remote and highland populations

It is essential for:

• Rimewheel cheese production  
• Frostcure meat systems  
• preservation of fish and dairy

Without Glacier Salt:

> Eryndor’s food systems would collapse within a season

• contamination risk from improper harvesting

• overharvesting can degrade salt flats  
• poor storage reduces effectiveness
