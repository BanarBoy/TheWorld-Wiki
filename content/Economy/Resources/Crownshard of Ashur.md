# Overview

The Crownshard of Ashur is the purest form of salt found in Thalassar, harvested from the blinding salt basins surrounding [[Ashur-Taal]] in the heart of the [[Glasswaste Basin]]. Formed from the long evaporation of ancient inland seas, these deposits crystallize into dense, translucent structures of exceptional purity.

Unlike common salt, Crownshard forms in large, angular crystals that can be broken, transported, and refined with minimal processing. Its clarity and mineral consistency make it uniquely suited not only for preservation, but also for industrial and artisanal uses—most notably in the production of Saltglass in Seluqar.

The resource is the foundation of long-distance survival. Without it, Banar spoils, meat rots, and trade collapses. With it, food travels across continents, armies sustain themselves, and economies extend far beyond their natural limits. Control of Crownshard is not simply economic—it is existential.

---

# Geographic Distribution

Crownshard deposits occur exclusively within:

- the central salt basins of the [[Glasswaste Basin]]
- the extraction fields surrounding [[Ashur-Taal]]
- deep mineral crust layers formed by ancient evaporated seas

The region’s extreme aridity and heat create ideal conditions for:

- large-scale crystallization
- minimal impurity formation
- dense, stable salt structures

No comparable deposits exist elsewhere in Thalassar, making the Glasswaste the sole global source of true Crownshard.

---

# Production

Extraction of Crownshard is harsh and tightly controlled.

The process includes:

- breaking large salt crusts into transportable shards
- excavating deeper crystal formations
- sorting by clarity and density

Production is centered around:

- **Ashur-Taal** — administrative and processing core
- surrounding salt fields — primary extraction zones

Labor is:

- highly regulated
- physically demanding
- often conducted under strict Malqaiah supervision

Only select portions of harvested salt qualify as **export-grade Crownshard**, distinguished by:

- size
- clarity
- structural integrity

---

# Processing and Transport

Crownshard requires minimal refinement but careful handling.

Processing includes:

- grading and sorting crystals
- breaking into standardized caravan loads
- packaging for transport

Transport is one of the most critical aspects:

- carried via heavily guarded caravan routes
- moved from [[Ashur-Taal]] to:
    - [[Mareth Vel]] (staging and redistribution)
    - [[Glasswater Reach]] (refinement and export)

Due to its value, shipments are:

- tightly tracked
- heavily protected
- often escorted by armed forces

---

# Economic Importance

Crownshard is one of the most strategically important resources in Thalassar.

It is essential for:

- food preservation (Banar, meats, travel rations)
- sustaining caravan and naval trade
- enabling long-distance logistics
- glassmaking industries in Seluqar

Key dependencies:

- the entire Thalassari food system relies on it for preservation
- Seluqar industries depend on it for Saltglass production
- caravan networks require it for survival

Without Crownshard, Thalassar’s interconnected economy would collapse.

---

# Controlling Powers

The **Malqaiah Clan** maintains absolute control over Crownshard.

Their power is enforced through:

- direct control of extraction sites in [[Ashur-Taal]]
- regulation of labor and production quotas
- strict oversight of caravan distribution

No significant quantity of Crownshard leaves the Glasswaste without Malqaiah approval.

This monopoly grants them immense leverage over:

- trade networks
- food systems
- rival powers

---

# Trade Networks

Crownshard moves through highly controlled routes:

- from [[Ashur-Taal]] to [[Mareth Vel]] via desert caravans
- onward to:
    - [[Glasswater Reach]] for refinement and export
    - inland regions through caravan networks

From Seluqar, it is distributed globally as:

- raw preservation salt
- refined products
- industrial material

Foreign markets treat Crownshard as both necessity and luxury.

---

# Cultural Significance

Within the Glasswaste, Crownshard represents:

- endurance
- control over scarcity
- survival through discipline

Among outsiders, it symbolizes:

- Thalassari reach
- the ability to preserve and transport life itself

In some traditions, pure crystals are kept as symbols of stability and permanence.

# Dangers and Limitations

- extraction in extreme environmental conditions
- dependence on a single geographic source
- vulnerability of caravan routes
- potential for monopolistic control

Any disruption to Crownshard supply has immediate and widespread consequences.
