# Merrowfen Grand Red

**Resource Type:** Luxury Agricultural Product / Prestige Wine  
**Primary Region(s):** [[Allacroix Vineyards]]  
**Major Production Centers:** [[Merrowfen]]  
**Controlling Groups:** [[House Allacroix]], vineyard estates, vintners’ guilds  
**Primary Uses:** Noble consumption, diplomatic feasts, luxury trade  
**Trade Value:** High / Prestige

---

# Overview

Merrowfen Grand Red is the signature wine of the renowned [[Allacroix Vineyards]] and one of the most widely respected red wines produced in Valecaryn. Known for its rich body, deep ruby color, and balanced structure, it has become a staple of noble banquets and prestigious gatherings across the continent.

Unlike the rare and limited [[Sunfall Reserve Wine]], Merrowfen Grand Red is produced in larger quantities each harvest. While still considered a luxury product, it is widely traded among noble houses, wealthy merchants, and influential guilds.

The reputation of Merrowfen Grand Red has helped establish the city of [[Merrowfen]] as one of the most influential wine centers in Valecaryn. Merchants regularly transport shipments of the wine to major cities and coastal ports, where it is sold in elite markets.

For many nobles, serving Merrowfen Grand Red at a feast represents a mark of sophistication and good taste.

---

# Geographic Origin

Merrowfen Grand Red is produced in the rolling vineyards surrounding [[Merrowfen]] within the region known as the [[Allacroix Vineyards]].

The area’s geography is particularly suited to growing high-quality red wine grapes due to:

• fertile river-enriched soils  
• gentle sunlit hills ideal for vineyards  
• mild seasonal weather patterns  
• cooling breezes from nearby waterways

These conditions allow grapes to develop strong flavors and balanced acidity, producing wines well suited for aging.

---

# Production

The production of Merrowfen Grand Red follows long-standing winemaking traditions refined over generations.

During harvest season, grapes are carefully selected from vineyards throughout the region. After harvesting, the fruit is transported to estate wineries where fermentation begins.

The wine is aged in oak barrels, allowing it to develop complexity and structure before being bottled and released to the market.

While some vintages are consumed relatively young, others are aged for several years before reaching noble cellars.

---

# Processing and Storage

After fermentation and barrel aging, Merrowfen Grand Red is bottled and stored in cellars across the city of [[Merrowfen]].

These cellars maintain stable conditions that allow the wine to mature gradually.

Many noble households purchase young vintages and age them further in private cellars, creating personal collections that grow in value over time.

---

# Economic Importance

Merrowfen Grand Red is one of the most important luxury exports produced in Valecaryn.

The wine trade supports:

• vineyard estates across the Merrowfen region  
• cooperages that produce wine barrels  
• merchant houses transporting wine to distant markets  
• cellar masters and vintners specializing in aging and storage

Because of its strong reputation, Merrowfen Grand Red commands high prices in both domestic and foreign markets.

---

# Trade Networks

Shipments of Merrowfen Grand Red travel throughout Valecaryn and beyond.

Major destinations include:

• noble courts in [[Aureth Caldras]]  
• luxury markets in port cities such as [[Velossa]] and [[Westport]]  
• merchant trade networks operating across the continent

Wine caravans frequently transport barrels from [[Merrowfen]] to major trade centers, while coastal shipping routes carry bottled vintages to distant markets.

---

# Cultural Significance

Merrowfen Grand Red is closely associated with noble hospitality and aristocratic culture.

Serving the wine at a gathering signals refinement and wealth, while gifting a bottle is often used to strengthen diplomatic relationships between powerful families.

Many noble houses maintain extensive wine cellars containing Merrowfen vintages, some of which are preserved for decades before being opened.

---

# Notable Locations

**[[Allacroix Vineyards]]**  
The expansive vineyard region where Merrowfen Grand Red is cultivated.

**[[Merrowfen]]**  
The central hub of wine production, storage, and trade for the region.

---

# Rumors and Conflicts

Some merchants claim that certain vineyard estates secretly blend grapes from lesser vineyards into their Merrowfen Grand Red vintages to increase production.

Others believe that rival merchant houses occasionally sabotage wine shipments in order to disrupt competitors.

There are also rumors that ancient vineyard plots near Merrowfen produce grapes capable of creating wines even finer than Sunfall Reserve.
