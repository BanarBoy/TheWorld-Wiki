# Amberfall White

**Resource Type:** Agricultural Product / Commercial Wine  
**Primary Region(s):** [[Ambervale Downs]]  
**Major Production Centers:** Vineyard estates throughout the Ambervale Downs  
**Controlling Groups:** [[House Thornweave]], vineyard estates, vintners’ guilds, wine merchant houses  
**Primary Uses:** Commercial trade, taverns, merchant households, export markets  
**Trade Value:** Common / Profitable Export

---

# Overview

Amberfall White is the most widely produced wine of the [[Ambervale Downs]] and serves as the commercial foundation of Thornweave’s wine trade. Produced in large quantities across the vineyard estates of the region, it is known for its clean, bright character and reliable quality.

While prestigious vintages such as [[Suncrest White]] and [[Amberfall Reserve]] dominate noble markets, Amberfall White is designed for broader consumption. Merchants, ship captains, and tavern keepers throughout Valecaryn regularly purchase barrels of the wine, making it one of the most common wines encountered in trade cities and ports.

Because of its dependable production and wide availability, Amberfall White has become a staple of the continent’s wine trade and a major export of the Ambervale region.

---

# Geographic Origin

Amberfall White originates from vineyards spread across the rolling hills of the [[Ambervale Downs]]. The region’s climate and terrain create excellent conditions for white wine grapes.

Key environmental features include:

• sunlit slopes ideal for vine cultivation  
• mineral-rich soils that support healthy vineyards  
• warm growing seasons moderated by coastal winds  
• gentle hills allowing large vineyard estates

These conditions allow for consistent harvests, enabling Thornweave estates to produce Amberfall White in substantial quantities each year.

---

# Production

Amberfall White is produced across numerous vineyard estates throughout the Ambervale Downs.

Grapes are harvested during the autumn season and transported to regional wineries where fermentation begins. The wine is typically fermented in large vats and aged for a shorter period than reserve wines, allowing it to reach the market relatively quickly.

This efficient production process allows Thornweave vintners to produce large volumes while maintaining reliable quality.

---

# Processing and Storage

After fermentation, Amberfall White is stored in large barrels and cellars throughout the Ambervale region before being transported to trade markets.

Because it is designed for widespread consumption, the wine is usually bottled or barreled and distributed within a year or two of harvest.

Large merchant houses purchase shipments of Amberfall White for resale throughout Valecaryn.

---

# Economic Importance

Amberfall White is one of the most important commercial exports produced by [[House Thornweave]].

The wine trade supports:

• vineyard laborers and estate workers  
• vintners and cellar masters  
• cooperages producing barrels  
• merchant houses transporting wine across the continent

Because of the scale of production, Amberfall White represents a major source of income for the Ambervale Downs.

---

# Trade Networks

Amberfall White travels widely across Valecaryn and beyond.

Major trade destinations include:

• merchant cities such as [[Lethryn Prime]]  
• coastal ports like [[Velossa]] and [[Westport]]  
• inland markets supplied by caravan trade routes

Barrels of Amberfall White are a common cargo aboard merchant ships departing the Coinward Coast.

---

# Cultural Significance

While not considered a noble prestige wine, Amberfall White holds an important place in everyday life across Valecaryn.

It is commonly served in taverns, merchant households, and aboard ships. Sailors, traders, and travelers throughout the continent are familiar with the wine’s crisp flavor and reliable quality.

For many people, Amberfall White is the wine most often encountered outside aristocratic circles.

---

# Notable Locations

**[[Ambervale Downs]]**  
The vineyard region where Amberfall White grapes are grown.

**Thornweave Estate Vineyards**  
Large estates that oversee the cultivation and production of Ambervale wines.

**[[Velossa]]**  
A nearby port city that serves as a major export hub for Ambervale wine shipments.

---

# Rumors and Conflicts

Some merchants claim that certain estates secretly divert their best Amberfall grapes into the production of reserve wines.

Others suspect that rival wine merchants occasionally attempt to counterfeit Ambervale wines in foreign markets.

There are also whispers that expanding vineyard estates may soon begin competing more directly with the famed vineyards of [[Merrowfen]].
