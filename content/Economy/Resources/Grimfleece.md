# Grimfleece

**Resource Type:** Livestock / Textile  
**Primary Region(s):** Northwestern highlands of [[Eryndor]] — [[Grimbarak]] uplands, [[Rimewatch]] ridges, western [[Crown of Winter]] slopes  
**Major Production Centers:** [[Grimbarak]], [[Rimewatch]], highland holds and seasonal grazing routes  
**Controlling Groups:** Highland clans, pastoral guilds, local jarldoms (northwestern Eryndor), trade intermediaries in [[Ravenfjord]]

**Trade Value:** Valuable

---

# Overview

Grimfleece is a dense, cold-resistant wool harvested from highland sheep breeds native to the harsh northwestern reaches of Eryndor. Bred over generations to survive relentless wind, ice, and thin grazing land, these animals produce a thick, tightly woven fleece that retains heat exceptionally well while resisting moisture and abrasion.

Unlike softer lowland wools, Grimfleece is slightly coarse and heavy, but extraordinarily durable. It is not prized for comfort—it is prized for **survival**. Cloaks, travel gear, and military garments made from Grimfleece are known to endure years of use in punishing conditions without significant degradation.

Across Eryndor, it forms the backbone of cold-weather life. Beyond its homeland, it is regarded as one of the most reliable textile exports from the north, especially valued in maritime and military contexts.

> Where silk adorns,  
> Grimfleece endures.

---

# Geographic Distribution

Grimfleece-producing herds are found almost exclusively in:

• the wind-scoured uplands around [[Grimbarak]]  
• the ridge systems near [[Rimewatch]]  
• the western slopes of the [[Crown of Winter]]

These regions are characterized by:

• cold, persistent winds  
• sparse, mineral-poor grazing land  
• steep, rugged terrain

The sheep that produce Grimfleece thrive where other livestock cannot. Their fleece develops its density and resilience specifically because of:

• constant exposure to cold stress  
• limited but hardy vegetation  
• seasonal migration across elevation gradients

Attempts to raise these animals outside these conditions have consistently resulted in inferior wool.

---

# Production

Grimfleece production is a combination of pastoral skill and environmental necessity.

Herding practices include:

• seasonal movement between low and high grazing zones  
• selective breeding for density and durability  
• careful timing of shearing to preserve fleece integrity

Shearing is:

• labor-intensive  
• done in short seasonal windows  
• often carried out in protected highland enclosures

Production is:

• moderate in volume  
• highly consistent in quality  
• dependent on stable herd management

The most skilled herders are able to produce fleece that is thicker, more uniform, and more resistant to wear.

# Processing and Transport

Raw Grimfleece is not immediately usable—it requires significant processing.

---

### Processing

Primary processing occurs in:

• [[Grimbarak]]  
• [[Rimewatch]]  
• smaller highland settlements

Steps include:

• cleaning and de-oiling the fleece  
• combing and aligning fibers  
• spinning into dense yarn  
• weaving into heavy cloth

Unlike finer textiles, Grimfleece is:

• tightly woven  
• minimally softened  
• often left intentionally rugged

---

### Transport

Grimfleece moves through:

• overland caravan routes from highlands to [[Ravenfjord]]  
• coastal shipping from northwestern ports  
• inland trade routes into southern Eryndor and beyond

Because of its weight, it is typically transported as:

• raw fleece (for local processing)  
• finished cloth in compressed bundles

---

# Economic Importance

Grimfleece is a **core survival resource** within Eryndor and a valuable export beyond it.

It is essential for:

• outfitting soldiers and scouts  
• sustaining highland populations  
• supporting maritime crews in cold regions

Cities such as [[Grimbarak]] and [[Rimewatch]] depend heavily on it, while trade hubs like [[Ravenfjord]] benefit from its export.

Though not rare, its reliability makes it indispensable.

---

# Controlling Powers

Grimfleece is controlled primarily at the local and regional level.

Key actors include:

• highland clans managing herds  
• pastoral guilds overseeing production standards  
• regional jarldoms coordinating trade and taxation

Control is less centralized than other resources, but influence comes from:

• access to grazing land  
• herd size and quality  
• control of trade routes

---

# Trade Networks

Grimfleece is exported across:

• southern Eryndor markets  
• Valecaryn trade cities  
• Seluqar maritime networks

It is especially valued in:

• naval outfitting  
• expeditionary gear  
• frontier settlements

While not a luxury good, it is widely traded due to its reliability.

---

# Cultural Significance

Grimfleece is deeply tied to highland identity.

It represents:

• endurance  
• self-reliance  
• adaptation to harsh environments

In the northwestern regions, wearing Grimfleece is not a status symbol—it is a **shared necessity**.

Well-crafted garments, however, may indicate:

• clan affiliation  
• regional origin  
• craftsmanship

---

# Dangers and Limitations

• dependence on harsh and specific environmental conditions  
• vulnerability of herds to extreme winters or disease  
• limited scalability due to terrain constraints  
• heavy weight increases transport difficulty

Additionally:

• overgrazing can damage fragile highland ecosystems  
• loss of herd lines reduces fleece quality over time
