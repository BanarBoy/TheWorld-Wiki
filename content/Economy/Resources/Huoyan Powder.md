# Overview

Huoyan Powder is a refined explosive compound developed and tightly controlled within Kiyora, representing one of the region’s most strategically significant technological resources. Produced through the careful combination of mineral and organic components, it generates a rapid ignition reaction capable of producing force, light, and concussive impact.

Unlike common materials, Huoyan Powder is not widely available. Its production is restricted to state-controlled facilities, and its distribution is managed directly by central authorities. While knowledge of its existence has spread beyond Kiyora, reliable production remains rare due to the precision required in its formulation.

Within Kiyora, Huoyan Powder is used in controlled applications—signal flares, excavation work, and specialized weaponry. Its military use is deliberate and limited, reflecting a broader philosophy of discipline and control rather than overwhelming force.

More than a weapon, Huoyan Powder represents the application of knowledge as power. Its existence reinforces the authority of the state, which maintains strict control over both its production and use.

# Geographic Distribution

Huoyan Powder is not naturally occurring but depends on materials sourced from:

• mineral deposits in interior Kiyoran regions  
• agricultural and organic inputs from surrounding provinces  
• controlled supply routes feeding into [[Laoshan]] within the **Laohe basin**

Production is concentrated in:

• secured facilities near [[Laoshan]]  
• restricted zones away from dense population centers  
• sites chosen for both access and containment

The need for:

• precise material inputs  
• controlled environments  
• secure oversight

limits production to a small number of state-approved locations.

# Production

Huoyan Powder production is highly controlled and hazardous.

---

### Material Preparation

• base materials are refined and purified before use  
• exact composition is closely guarded  
• impurities can destabilize the compound

---

### Mixing

• materials are combined under controlled conditions  
• ratios determine strength, burn rate, and stability  
• improper handling can trigger accidental ignition

---

### Stabilization

• finished powder is dried and conditioned  
• stored in sealed containers to prevent degradation  
• batches are tested before release

Production is:

• dangerous  
• knowledge-dependent  
• strictly regulated

# Processing and Transport

---

### Processing

Huoyan Powder is prepared in graded forms:

• low-strength mixtures for signaling and controlled use  
• standard-grade powder for industrial and military application  
• refined blends for specialized or experimental purposes

It may also be:

• pre-packed into charges  
• incorporated into devices  
• issued in measured quantities

---

### Transport

Due to volatility, Huoyan Powder is transported:

• in sealed, reinforced containers  
• under armed and supervised escort  
• along secured routes between [[Laoshan]] (Laohe basin) and [[Jinshan]] (Jinghe basin)

Shipments are:

• tracked  
• limited in volume  
• carefully scheduled

Huoyan Powder is a **strategic resource**, not a commercial commodity.

• enables advanced engineering and controlled demolition

• provides a tactical advantage in military applications

Its value lies not in quantity, but in:

> control, precision, and restricted access

• **central state authorities of [[Jinshan]]** — regulate all production and distribution

• **licensed alchemical guilds** — maintain technical processes

• illegal  
• heavily punished  
• treated as a threat to state stability
