# Valecaryni Crowns

**Currency Name:** Valecaryni Crowns  
**Type:** Coin (Metal-Based)  
**Primary Region(s):** [[Valecaryn]]  
**Issuing Authority:** Royal Mint of [[Aureth Caldras]] / Crown Authority of Valecaryn  
**Backing System:** Intrinsic Metal Value (Copper / Silver / Gold by weight)  
**Denominations:** Copper Crowns, Silver Crowns, Gold Crowns (standardized weight tiers)  
**Relative Value:** Stable → High-Value (Gold)  
**Convertibility:** Universally accepted; exchangeable in all major regions including [[Hinora]]

---

# Overview

Valecaryni Crowns are the dominant coin-based currency of the known world, valued for their consistency, reliability, and intrinsic worth. Minted in standardized weights of copper, silver, and gold, each coin carries value not just by decree, but by the material from which it is made.

Produced under strict oversight in the capital of [[Aureth Caldras]], the Crowns are trusted across continents because they can be weighed, tested, and verified independently of any governing authority. This makes them especially valuable in long-distance trade, where trust in foreign systems may be limited.

Unlike paper or credit-based systems, Valecaryni Crowns require no bureaucracy to function. Their value is immediate and tangible, making them the preferred currency for merchants, mercenaries, and international trade networks.

Across the world, they are not just accepted—they are expected.

Valecaryni Crowns are:

• struck with precise weight standards  
• marked with official Valecaryni insignia

• the seal of the Crown of Valecaryn  
• mint markings indicating origin and batch  
• ridged or patterned edges to prevent shaving

Each denomination is:

• uniform in weight and composition  
• easily distinguishable by size and color

Higher-value coins (gold):

• often feature more intricate designs  
• may include additional verification markings

# Issuance and Control

Valecaryni Crowns are minted through a centralized system.

• produced in the Royal Mint of [[Aureth Caldras]]  
• overseen directly by the Valecaryni Crown  
• distributed through state-sanctioned channels

Currency enters circulation through:

• trade payments  
• taxation redistribution  
• military and state expenditures

Control mechanisms include:

• strict metal purity standards  
• controlled minting processes  
• penalties for debasement or unauthorized minting

# Relationship with Other Currencies

Valecaryni Crowns interact directly with other systems, especially Zhilan Notes.

• widely accepted within [[Kiyora]]  
• often converted into Zhilan Notes for internal use

Exchange dynamics:

• Crowns → Zhilan Notes upon entering Kiyora  
• Zhilan Notes → Crowns when leaving

Outside Kiyora:

• Crowns dominate over most local currencies  
• often replace weaker or less stable systems

# Backing and Trust System

Valecaryni Crowns are backed by:

• the intrinsic value of their metal content  
• standardized weight and purity  
• reputation of Valecaryni minting practices

Trust is maintained through:

• consistency of production  
• ease of independent verification  
• long-standing global acceptance

If trust fails:

• coins are weighed and tested directly  
• value reverts to raw metal worth

# Regulation and Enforcement

Enforcement is strict and widely recognized.

Measures include:

• precise minting standards  
• anti-shaving edge designs  
• inspection of coins in major markets

Penalties for violations:

• severe punishment for counterfeiting  
• execution or exile for large-scale debasement  
• confiscation of fraudulent currency

Responsible authorities:

• Royal Mint of [[Aureth Caldras]]  
• Crown inspectors  
• trade guild verification systems
