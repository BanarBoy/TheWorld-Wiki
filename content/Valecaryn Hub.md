# Overview

Valecaryn is the western continent and the heart of the kingdom that bears its name, defined by interconnected river systems, fertile agricultural regions, and powerful maritime trade networks. From the inland grain fields of the [[Gold Marches]] to the major ports of [[Lethryn Prime]] and [[Westport]], the continent functions as a tightly interwoven system of production, transport, and exchange.

Following the end of the [[Crown Crisis]], Valecaryn exists under the restored authority of [[King Drosen]]. However, power is distributed across noble houses, merchant dynasties, and institutional authorities, each exerting influence over different aspects of the realm.

The result is a stable but competitive system—one where control of land, trade, and information determines power more than titles alone.

# Power Structure

Valecaryn’s stability depends on a balance between interconnected powers:

**The Crown**  
• [[King Drosen]]

**Noble Houses**  
• land, military, and regional control

**Merchant Houses**  
• trade, shipping, and economic systems

**Institutional Authority**  
• [[House Vaul]]  
• [[Meridian Compact]]

No single force dominates completely—power is maintained through balance.
