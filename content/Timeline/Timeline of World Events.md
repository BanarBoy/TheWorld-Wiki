**Valecaryn**
590: Birth of Ansittar the Unifier
620-650: Wars of Unification unite the river valleys of Valecaryn
650: Ansittar crowned first King of Valecaryn
720: Reign of Lochvar the Great begins and Valecaryn expands beyond original valleys
790: Reign of Yseverin the Bold; eastern invasion defeated at Battle of the Crownfields
850: Caldran I issues the Caldran Code, establishing royal law
890: Caldran II expands royal roads, fortifications, and infrastructure
930: Aurelion the Founder establishes Aureth Caldras as the capital
930-970: Golden Age of Valecaryn
1020: Death of Seredain the Last Crown King
1020-1510: Crown Crisis
1510: Drosen crowns himself King
1522-1530: The Corsair Wars
1540: Present Day
