---

# Overview

Alcindor the Principled is one of the most dangerous living Sylraen—not because of his power alone, but because of his certainty.

Where most Sylraen live in ignorance or quiet avoidance of their lineage, Alcindor has devoted his life to understanding it. Through extensive study of the ruins of [[Old Sylrae]] and the scattered remnants of Sylraen history, he has arrived at a stark conclusion:

> **The Sylraen cannot be allowed to gather again.**

Unlike others who internalize this belief as restraint, Alcindor acts on it with deliberate purpose. He seeks out Sylraen who demonstrate strong resonance—especially those who risk forming communities—and eliminates them before they can become a threat.

To those who encounter him outside this mission, he appears calm, disciplined, and almost monastic. To those he has judged, he is inevitable.

---

# Early Life

Little is known of Alcindor’s early life, including where he was born or who raised him. Like many Sylraen, he likely grew up unaware of his lineage.

What is known is that his awakening was not gradual.

At some point in early adulthood, Alcindor’s connection manifested with unusual clarity and intensity. Rather than suppressing it, he pursued understanding. This led him to the ruins of Sylrae, where he spent years studying the remnants of the fallen civilization.

It was there that his worldview took shape—not through myth, but through interpretation of what remained.

---

# Rise to Prominence

Alcindor did not rise through institutions or public recognition. His reputation spread quietly, through absence.

- Sylraen who had begun to draw attention disappeared
    
- rumors of a “silent elf” following patterns others could not see
    
- fragments of research and recovered knowledge began circulating anonymously
    

Over time, a pattern emerged:

> Where Sylraen power began to concentrate, Alcindor appeared.

Those few who survived encounters with him describe a man who does not act in anger or haste, but with precise, deliberate judgment.

---

# Personality

Alcindor is defined by discipline.

He is:

- calm
    
- measured
    
- emotionally controlled
    
- unwavering in conviction
    

He does not appear cruel, nor does he take satisfaction in what he does. His actions are not driven by hatred, but by belief.

Outside of his mission, he presents as:

- quiet
    
- observant
    
- almost ascetic
    

He avoids excess, speaks little, and carries himself with the stillness of someone who has already resolved every internal conflict.

To Alcindor, there is no contradiction in his actions.

> **He does not see himself as a killer—he sees himself as a safeguard.**

---

# Skills and Abilities

Alcindor is an exceptionally powerful Sylraen, with a level of control rarely seen in the modern era.

He excels in:

• deep resonance with natural systems  
• stabilizing or disrupting environmental forces  
• sensing the presence of other Sylraen  
• maintaining internal control under extreme conditions  
• patient tracking over long periods

Unlike less experienced Sylraen, he does not allow his connection to surge uncontrollably. His power is tightly contained, released only when necessary.

# Relationships

**Belladonna — Emerging Threat**  
Alcindor would view Belladonna as extremely dangerous: untrained, powerful, and already influencing events beyond her understanding. She represents exactly the kind of risk he seeks to eliminate.

**[[Meridian Compact]] — Parallel Interest**  
While not aligned, their goals may occasionally overlap. The Compact seeks control; Alcindor seeks prevention. Each would view the other as useful—but ultimately incompatible.

**Other Sylraen — Subjects, Not Kin**  
Alcindor does not recognize kinship with other Sylraen. He sees only degrees of risk.

He operates entirely outside political systems.
