# Grand Counting Hall

**Type:** Guildhall / Financial Institution / Administrative Citadel  
**Location:** [[Geldmere]] — Financial District  
**Owner:** [[House Merrow]]  
**Founded:** Early consolidation of merchant finance (~200+ years ago)  
**Primary Function:** Central banking operations, credit issuance, and financial record management  
**Status:** Active

---

# Overview

The Grand Counting Hall is the headquarters of [[House Merrow]] and the most powerful financial institution in Valecaryn. From within its vast halls, credit is issued, debts are recorded, and the flow of wealth across the realm is measured, regulated, and enforced.

Unlike the discreet systems of [[House Ryn]], the Counting Hall is **visible authority**. Its scale, order, and permanence reinforce the legitimacy of Merrow’s financial dominance. Merchants, nobles, and institutions rely on its ledgers to validate transactions that shape economies across continents.

Every major financial action—loans, trade credit, insurance, large-scale investment—passes through or is influenced by the systems housed here.

> The Counting Hall does not move wealth.  
> It defines what wealth is allowed to do.

---

# Structure and Layout

The Grand Counting Hall is a monumental structure designed to embody stability, scale, and absolute control.

• constructed from polished stone, reinforced vaultwork, and fine architectural glass  
• towering façade overlooking Geldmere’s financial district  
• symmetrical, imposing design reflecting order and balance  
• large internal halls capable of housing hundreds of clerks and officials

Key sections include:

• **The Great Ledger Hall** — an immense chamber where active financial records are maintained and updated  
• **The Credit Assembly Floor** — where loans, trade financing, and large-scale agreements are structured  
• **The Vault Descent** — secured access points leading to underground treasury systems  
• **The Auditor’s Wing** — offices responsible for verifying accounts and preventing fraud  
• **The High Counting Chamber** — restricted council space where major financial decisions are made

The design emphasizes:

> **scale, clarity, and the inevitability of record**

# Notable Figures

**[[Kethra Merrow]] — Head of House Merrow**  
Directs the highest level of financial strategy and authority within the Counting Hall, overseeing credit systems, major agreements, and the broader economic posture of Valecaryn.

---

**[[Thalia Merrow]] — Keeper of the Old Ledgers**  
Custodian of the Counting Hall’s oldest and most sensitive financial records, Thalia oversees the preservation and interpretation of legacy accounts that predate modern systems. These ledgers contain long-standing debts, dormant obligations, and historical agreements whose relevance is not always obvious—but often decisive.

Her role is not merely archival. When disputes arise that cannot be resolved through current records, it is Thalia who determines whether the past still holds authority over the present.
