# Sarnhold

**Type:** Trade Hub / Agricultural Capital  
**Continent:** [[Valecaryn]]  
**Region:** [[Gold Marches]]  
**River / Coast:** Inland plains along regional caravan routes  
**Population:** ~110,000  
**Elevation:** Lowland plains  
**Government:** Regional governor with strong influence from agricultural guilds and ranching houses  
**Founded:** Early expansion period of the Valecaryni kingdom  
**Strategic Role:** Agricultural capital of the Gold Marches and central hub of the continent’s grain and livestock trade

---

# Overview

Sarnhold is the largest city of the [[Gold Marches]] and the economic heart of Valecaryn’s agricultural frontier. Surrounded by vast fields of grain and enormous grazing lands, the city serves as the primary market through which the bounty of the Marches flows to the rest of the continent.

The region’s famous products — including [[Goldenbar Wheat]], [[Sunmarch Grain]], [[Goldhorn Beef]], and [[Sarnhold Gold]] — all pass through the city’s markets before being shipped across Valecaryn. Livestock caravans, grain merchants, and ranching families regularly gather in Sarnhold to negotiate contracts and organize seasonal cattle drives.

Unlike the stone-built austerity of cities such as [[Stonebridge]], Sarnhold feels open and expansive. Broad roads, large livestock markets, and sprawling warehouses dominate the urban landscape, reflecting the agricultural wealth that sustains the city.

Travelers often describe Sarnhold as a city of wide horizons and loud markets — a place where the wealth of the plains is measured not in coins alone, but in herds, harvests, and grain shipments.

---

# Physical Geography

## Regional Context

Sarnhold sits in the center of the fertile plains known as the [[Gold Marches]], one of the most productive agricultural regions in Valecaryn.

The surrounding land stretches outward in rolling fields and open grazing country, with distant hills marking the boundaries of the region. The fertile soil and relatively gentle climate make the area ideal for large-scale grain farming and cattle ranching.

The city developed naturally at the intersection of several major caravan routes connecting the agricultural hinterlands to the larger markets of Valecaryn.

To the north lie the foothills leading toward the [[Regions/Blackspine Mountains]], while the open plains continue southward toward the major population centers of the continent.

---

## Immediate Terrain

The terrain surrounding Sarnhold is dominated by wide plains and gently rolling farmland.

Fields of grain stretch for miles beyond the city walls, while vast pasturelands support enormous herds of [[Goldhorn Cattle]]. Large ranch estates dot the countryside, each controlling grazing lands and livestock operations.

The city itself is built on relatively flat terrain, allowing it to expand outward rather than upward. Wide roads and large open squares accommodate the constant movement of livestock caravans and grain wagons.

---

## Hydrology

Several small rivers and irrigation channels run through the surrounding farmland, supplying water for crops and livestock.

These waterways help sustain the region’s agricultural productivity and allow farmers to maintain large harvests even during drier seasons.

Seasonal rains replenish groundwater and support the vast fields that surround the city.

---

## Climate

Sarnhold experiences a warm temperate plains climate well suited to agriculture.

**Spring**  
The growing season begins with moderate rainfall and warming temperatures.

**Summer**  
Long sunny days allow crops to mature, while ranchers move herds across open pasture.

**Autumn**  
Harvest season brings enormous activity as grain shipments and livestock drives arrive in the city.

**Winter**  
Winters are cool but rarely severe, allowing livestock to remain on pasture with minimal shelter.

# Ecology

## Flora

### Native Vegetation

Before large-scale farming, the Gold Marches were dominated by tall prairie grasses and scattered groves of hardy trees.

Remaining native vegetation includes:

• tall prairie grasses  
• riverbank willow and alder  
• scattered oak groves  
• wildflower meadows during spring

Much of the original ecosystem has been transformed into agricultural land.

---

### Agricultural Landscape

The landscape surrounding Sarnhold is dominated by cultivated farmland.

Major features include:

• enormous grain fields growing [[Goldenbar Wheat]] and [[Sunmarch Grain]]  
• ranchlands supporting [[Goldhorn Cattle]]  
• dairy farms producing milk used for [[Sarnhold Gold]]  
• large storage barns and granaries

These agricultural systems form the backbone of the region’s economy.

---

### Economically Important Plants

Key crops produced in the region include:

• [[Goldenbar Wheat]]  
• [[Sunmarch Grain]]  
• grazing grasses used for cattle

These crops supply both local consumption and export markets across the continent.

---

## Fauna

### Common Wildlife

Wildlife in the plains includes:

• deer  
• prairie foxes  
• wild hares  
• hawks and other birds of prey

Many animals live along the edges of farmland and river valleys.

---

### Predators and Dangerous Creatures

Occasional wolves roam the more remote parts of the plains, though they rarely approach populated ranching areas.

Severe weather and livestock stampedes are often more dangerous than predators.

---

### Domesticated Animals

The most important animals raised around Sarnhold include:

• [[Goldhorn Cattle]]  
• draft horses used in farming and cattle drives  
• sheep and goats on smaller farms  
• chickens kept for eggs and meat

Massive cattle herds moving through the plains are one of the defining sights of the region.
# Urban Structure

## City Layout

Sarnhold is a sprawling plains city designed around trade, livestock movement, and agricultural markets. Unlike older fortified cities with tight medieval streets, Sarnhold is built with **broad roads and large open squares** capable of handling massive wagons, livestock caravans, and seasonal harvest traffic.

The city grew outward from a central market district where early farmers and ranchers gathered to sell their produce. Over time, this market expanded into enormous livestock yards and grain exchanges that now dominate the city center.

Wide caravan roads extend from the city gates into the surrounding plains, connecting ranch estates, farming communities, and distant trade routes across Valecaryn.

Because the terrain is flat, Sarnhold has expanded horizontally into multiple large districts separated by broad avenues and market squares.

---

## Districts

### Grand Livestock Market

The largest and most famous district of Sarnhold is its enormous livestock market.

Thousands of animals may pass through the market during peak seasons, especially during major cattle drives. Ranchers from across the [[Gold Marches]] bring herds of [[Goldhorn Cattle]] to the city, where buyers negotiate contracts for meat, dairy, and breeding stock.

The district contains large fenced yards, auction platforms, and caravan staging areas.

---

### Grain Exchange District

The Grain Exchange District serves as the financial center of Sarnhold’s agricultural trade.

Massive warehouses and granaries store shipments of [[Goldenbar Wheat]] and [[Sunmarch Grain]] before they are transported across the continent. Merchant houses maintain offices here where contracts for grain shipments are negotiated.

The central grain exchange building is one of the busiest economic institutions in the Gold Marches.

---

### Ranchers’ Quarter

Large ranching families maintain estates and offices in this district.

The Ranchers’ Quarter houses the headquarters of many influential cattle families who control vast grazing lands across the plains. These families play an important role in the political and economic life of Sarnhold.

Wide courtyards and large stables dominate the architecture of the district.

---

### Artisan District

The Artisan District houses craftsmen who support the agricultural economy.

Important trades here include:

• wagon builders  
• leatherworkers using hides from [[Goldhorn Cattle]]  
• butchers and meat processors  
• cheesemakers producing [[Sarnhold Gold]]

The district is filled with workshops, markets, and small guild halls.

---

### Old Town

The oldest part of Sarnhold lies near the city’s original settlement site.

Here narrow streets and older stone buildings remain from the city’s early days as a small frontier market town. Taverns, inns, and small markets cater to travelers and caravan workers passing through the city.

---

## Architecture

Sarnhold’s architecture reflects its agricultural prosperity and practical needs.

Buildings are typically constructed from timber and stone, with wide courtyards and storage areas designed to support large-scale trade.

Common features include:

• broad wooden barns and granaries  
• stone warehouses built for grain storage  
• large stable complexes for livestock  
• long market halls used for trade negotiations

The overall style is sturdy and functional rather than ornate, reflecting the practical character of the region.

---

# Infrastructure

## Roads and Transportation

Sarnhold is the central transportation hub of the [[Gold Marches]].

Major caravan roads radiate outward from the city toward:

• [[Aureth Caldras]] and the central plains  
• the foothills of the [[Regions/Blackspine Mountains]]  
• merchant routes leading toward the coast

These roads carry constant traffic of grain wagons, cattle drives, and merchant caravans.

Large staging grounds outside the city walls allow caravans to assemble before departing.

---

## Water Systems

Water for the city is supplied through wells, nearby streams, and irrigation channels that also support surrounding farmland.

Many ranch estates maintain their own wells and reservoirs to supply livestock and agricultural operations.

Water management is particularly important during dry seasons to ensure adequate supplies for both people and animals.

---

## Food Supply

Although Sarnhold produces enormous quantities of food, much of the region’s agricultural output is exported.

Local food sources include:

• grain grown in surrounding farmland  
• beef and dairy products from [[Goldhorn Cattle]]  
• cheese such as [[Sarnhold Gold]]  
• vegetables and small farm produce

The city’s markets are famously well supplied with food.

---

## Defenses

Because Sarnhold sits in the relatively peaceful heart of the Gold Marches, its defenses are practical rather than imposing.

Defensive features include:

• modest city walls  
• gatehouses controlling major roads  
• watchtowers monitoring surrounding plains

The open terrain surrounding the city provides clear visibility of approaching threats.

---

# Economy

## Major Industries

Sarnhold’s economy is built entirely around agriculture and livestock trade.

Major industries include:

• cattle ranching  
• grain farming  
• meat processing  
• dairy production  
• wagon construction and livestock equipment

The city’s markets process enormous volumes of agricultural goods every year.

---

## Trade

Sarnhold exports some of the most important agricultural resources produced in Valecaryn.

Major exports include:

• [[Goldenbar Wheat]]  
• [[Sunmarch Grain]]  
• [[Goldhorn Beef]]  
• [[Sarnhold Gold]]

These products are transported by caravan to cities across the continent and eventually to coastal ports for export.

---

## Markets and Commerce

The markets of Sarnhold are among the busiest in Valecaryn.

The most important institutions include:

• the **Grand Livestock Market**  
• the **Grain Exchange**  
• the **Ranchers’ Council Hall**

Merchants, ranchers, and agricultural traders regularly gather in these markets to negotiate contracts and organize large shipments.

---

## Labor and Guilds

Several guilds and trade organizations influence the economy of Sarnhold.

Important groups include:

• the **Ranchers’ Council**  
• the **Grain Merchants’ Guild**  
• the **Butchers’ Guild**  
• the **Wagonmakers’ Guild**

These organizations regulate trade practices and maintain standards for agricultural products.
