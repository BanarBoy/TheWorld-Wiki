# Overview

The _Second Dividend_ is a heavily built carrack operating under the authority of [[House Voss]], designed to transport high-value cargo through the contested waters of [[Sapphire Reach]]. Built for endurance and capacity rather than speed, it represents a deliberate investment—used when the value of a shipment justifies significant risk.

Unlike most merchant vessels, the _Second Dividend_ regularly operates under enhanced security conditions. For particularly dangerous routes, House Voss contracts external specialists to reinforce the ship’s defensive capabilities rather than permanently militarizing its structure.

During its current voyage, the vessel carries three such specialists: [[Hadrian Garamond]], [[Kessarin Draeven]], and [[Torren Brackmoor]]. While none are members of the ship’s permanent crew, their authority is recognized for the duration of the mission. This creates a functional but temporary command structure layered over the ship’s normal hierarchy.

# Design and Construction

## Hull

• broad, deep hull designed for maximum cargo capacity  
• constructed from heavy hardwoods with reinforced sections incorporating [[Brinewood]]  
• iron-banded framing for structural integrity

The hull prioritizes:

• cargo volume  
• long-range stability  
• resistance to environmental and structural stress

---

## Rigging and Sails

• square-rigged configuration optimized for sustained travel  
• multi-sail system requiring coordinated crew operation  
• rigging composed of durable [[Vireline]]

This favors:

• consistency over speed  
• efficient long-distance trade movement

---

## Deck Layout

• multi-deck structure including cargo hold, main deck, and command areas  
• cargo secured below deck with organized access points  
• main deck arranged for both sailing operations and defensive response

Command during the current voyage is layered:

• permanent ship crew operating under standard officers  
• contracted marine unit under [[Kessarin Draeven]]  
• contracted artillery command under [[Torren Brackmoor]]  
• overall operational authority temporarily exercised by [[Hadrian Garamond]]

This structure is understood to be temporary and mission-specific.

---

## Materials and Craftsmanship

• sealed with refined [[Blackgum]] for water resistance  
• reinforced joints for long-term durability  
• construction emphasizes endurance over maneuverability
