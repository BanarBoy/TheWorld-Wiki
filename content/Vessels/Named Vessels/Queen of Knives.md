# Queen of Knives

**Type:** Cutter  
**Class:** Light Raider / Precision Interceptor  
**Flag:** [[Seressa Fairwind]]  
**Home Port:** [[Corsair’s Rest]]  
**Captain:** [[Seressa Fairwind]]  
**Crew Size:** ~40–60  
**Length:** ~70–85 ft  
**Draft:** Shallow  
**Propulsion:** Sail (Fore-and-aft rig)  
**Primary Role:** Rapid interception, precision strike, command vessel

---

# Overview

The _Queen of Knives_ is the flagship of [[Seressa Fairwind]], a cutter built for speed, control, and decisive engagement. Unlike larger warships that rely on weight of fire or intimidation, the vessel is designed to close distance quickly, strike with precision, and disengage before an opponent can recover.

Its silhouette is unmistakable: a low black hull, sharply raked forward, with three lanterns set low and tight along the bow. Among those familiar with the waters of [[Sapphire Reach]], the sight of those lights is often the only warning before engagement.

The ship reflects Seressa herself—efficient, controlled, and without excess. It is not built to endure prolonged battle, but to ensure that such battles never occur.

---

# Design and Construction

## Hull

• narrow, low-profile hull optimized for speed  
• aggressive forward rake to cut through chop and reduce drag  
• constructed using a blend of dense hardwoods reinforced with [[Brinewood]] along stress lines

The hull sits lower in the water than most vessels of its class, reducing visibility and making it harder to track at distance.

---

## Rigging and Sails

• fore-and-aft rigged for tight maneuvering and rapid adjustment  
• rigging composed of high-quality [[Vireline]] for durability under strain  
• sail configuration favors responsiveness over maximum power

This allows:

• quick directional changes  
• efficient operation with a smaller crew  
• strong performance in variable winds

---

## Deck Layout

• single primary deck with minimal obstruction  
• open working space optimized for rapid crew movement  
• limited cargo capacity in favor of combat readiness

Crew positioning is deliberate:

• boarding teams staged forward  
• command visibility maintained at all times

---

## Materials and Craftsmanship

• hull seams sealed with refined [[Blackgum]] for maximum water resistance  
• joints reinforced for high-speed stress tolerance  
• construction favors precision over redundancy

The vessel requires regular maintenance but performs at a higher level than most ships of similar size.

---

# Performance

## Speed

Exceptionally fast for its size.

• capable of closing distance quickly  
• performs best in moderate to strong wind conditions

---

## Maneuverability

Highly responsive.

• tight turning radius  
• rapid sail adjustment  
• effective in both open water and constrained environments

---

## Endurance

Moderate.

• limited cargo and supply storage  
• not intended for extended independent voyages

Crew relies on efficient resupply and disciplined operation.

---

## Strengths and Weaknesses

**Strengths:**

• speed and interception capability  
• precise maneuvering  
• low visibility profile  
• disciplined crew coordination

**Weaknesses:**

• limited durability in prolonged engagements  
• low cargo capacity  
• vulnerable if outnumbered or pinned

---

# Armament

• light cannon complement (limited number, strategically placed)  
• swivel guns for anti-personnel use  
• boarding equipment including grapples and short-range firearms

The ship is not designed for sustained bombardment. Its armament supports **engagement, not siege**.
