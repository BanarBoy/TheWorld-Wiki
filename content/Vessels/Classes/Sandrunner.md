# Sandrunner

**Type:** Merchant / Military Hybrid  
**Primary Use:** High-speed outbound transport of goods and force projection from Seluqar  
**Associated Regions:** [[Seluqar]], Southwestern [[Thalassar]]  
**Associated Routes / Currents:** [[Sand Current]], secondary interaction with [[Golden Flow]] (arrival phase)  
**Typical Operators:** Seluqar merchant fleets, Thalassari naval command, royal trade authorities  
**Defining Trait:** A launch-optimized vessel designed to exploit the Sand Current, trading return efficiency for **unmatched outbound speed and economic throughput**

---

# Overview

The Sandrunner is the defining vessel of Seluqar’s maritime dominance—a ship class engineered specifically to exploit the immense force of the Sand Current. Where most ships are designed for balance, the Sandrunner is deliberately asymmetric in purpose: it is built to **leave**, not to return.

Originating from the shipyards of Seluqar, these vessels are constructed to align immediately with the Sand Current upon departure, allowing them to achieve rapid acceleration and maintain high-speed transit across open water toward Valecaryn. This efficiency in outbound movement transformed Seluqar into a global trade powerhouse, enabling faster delivery cycles and greater volume throughput than any competing port.

However, this advantage comes with a cost. Sandrunners are not designed for reverse travel against the current that empowers them. Instead, they operate as part of a larger maritime circuit, relying on alternative routes—such as the Zhar Return and Monsoon Drift—to complete their journeys.

To sail a Sandrunner is to commit to a system:

> **departure defines the vessel—return defines the strategy**

---

# Design Philosophy

The Sandrunner is built around a single principle:

> **maximize outbound velocity at all costs**

This results in:

• prioritization of forward momentum over maneuverability  
• structural reinforcement along stress lines created by current acceleration  
• reduced emphasis on versatility or adaptability  
• acceptance of inefficiency outside intended use

It is not a general-purpose ship—it is a **specialized economic instrument**

---

# Structural Characteristics

• Long, narrow hull optimized for forward flow alignment  
• Reinforced bow and keel to withstand sustained current pressure  
• Lightweight upper structure to reduce drag  
• Cargo holds designed for dense, high-value packing rather than bulk sprawl  
• Sail configuration optimized for initial alignment, not long-term adaptability

The ship is built to:

> **enter the current cleanly and stay aligned**

---

# Performance Profile

• Extremely fast when aligned with the Sand Current  
• Rapid acceleration immediately after departure  
• Limited maneuverability at peak speed  
• Poor efficiency outside of current-assisted travel  
• Reduced performance when forced into variable or opposing conditions

In ideal conditions:

> **it outpaces nearly everything on the water**

---

# Operational Role

Sandrunners function as:

• primary export vessels for Seluqar’s trade economy  
• rapid-response naval projection ships  
• high-value cargo carriers for time-sensitive goods

They are rarely used independently in unknown waters. Instead, they operate within:

• structured departure cycles  
• coordinated trade waves  
• protected or monitored outbound corridors

---

# Strengths

• unmatched outbound speed  
• efficient use of the Sand Current  
• high economic throughput  
• ability to deliver goods faster than competitors  
• strong structural integrity under directional stress

---

# Limitations

• inefficient or impractical for return travel  
• reduced maneuverability at speed  
• reliance on specific current conditions  
• vulnerability if forced out of optimal alignment  
• limited adaptability outside intended route

> A Sandrunner outside its system is at a disadvantage

---

# Relationship to Currents

The Sandrunner is inseparable from the Sand Current.

• designed specifically to align with its flow  
• gains maximum efficiency only within it  
• cannot replicate its performance in other currents  
• depends on secondary currents (Zhar Return, Monsoon Drift) for full voyage cycles

This is not a ship that uses a current:

> **it exists because of one**

---

# Crew and Operation

• highly trained navigators specializing in current alignment  
• disciplined crew trained for high-speed operations  
• strong coordination required during launch phase  
• cultural emphasis on timing, precision, and discipline

Seluqar crews treat departure as a practiced, near-ritual process.

---

# Economic Role

The Sandrunner is the backbone of Seluqar’s economy.

• enables rapid export cycles  
• supports high-value, high-frequency trade  
• outcompetes slower merchant systems  
• reinforces Seluqar’s position as a dominant trade hub

It turns geography into:

> **sustained economic advantage**

---

# Strategic Role

• allows Seluqar to project power outward quickly  
• enables rapid deployment of naval assets  
• reinforces dominance over trade routes connected to Valecaryn  
• creates dependency on Seluqar-origin goods and timing

It is both:

• a merchant vessel  
• and a strategic weapon
